About Project

Navigate Mumbai efficiently with real-time data and multi-modal transport options. This Intelligent Multi-Modal Route Discovery system demonstrates the use of complex logic engines for city-specific transport rules.

Smart Routing

Uses geospatial algorithms (Haversine Formula) to calculate precise distances while accounting for road tortuosity.

Policy Logic

Includes built-in awareness of local rules, such as the specific auto-rickshaw restrictions in South Mumbai.

Dynamic Fares

Real-time cost estimation for Train, Metro, Bus, and Cabs based on the latest 2024 government rate cards.

Digital Booking

Features a fully simulated payment gateway with instant UPI QR generation for end-to-end ticketing.
